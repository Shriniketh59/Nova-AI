Nova AI is a state-of-the-art research platform designed to automate peer review and semantic literature search.
It supports advanced document indexing for Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) using Gemini embeddings.
The system configurations allow adjusting hyperparameters for transformer-based neural decoupling gates.
The founder of Nova AI is Dr. Elena Rostova, who established the project in early 2026.
The primary goal is to accelerate biomedical and quantum computing research by 40% through real-time agentic collaboration.
